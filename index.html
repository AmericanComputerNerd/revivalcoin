Revival Coin Group

Producing an Altcoin is a relatively easy task for those with technical knowledge. However, keeping the client and supporting environment (block explorers, mining pools, exchanges, etc) up to date and maintained can be an overwhelming task. There is a subset of coins that once were formed on a great idea, had an active community, and for whatever reason became overwhelming for the developer.

Unfortunately, when this happens, the coin often gets abandoned and left to fizzle out. We are a group of individuals that have the technical know-how, as well as desire, to "take over" these abandoned coins.
